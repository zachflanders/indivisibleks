Our Goals

  • To urge our representatives to resist Trumpism.
  • To hold our representatives accountable when they support or enable Trumpism and remove them from office.
  • To identify, support, and elect representatives who resist Trumpism


What We Stand For

  • We are a grassroots movement formed to resist Trumpism through peaceful, democratic means.
  • We reject the politics of division. We link arms with all our brothers and sisters who peacefully resist Trumpism.
  • We cherish and defend American democratic norms and ideals as enshrined in the Constitution and 250 years of tradition.
  • We believe in respect and equality for all people.
